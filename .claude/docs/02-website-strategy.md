# Website Strategy

## Website Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **Company showcase** — Present Softgrid Sensing as a credible, technology-driven platform company
2. **Customer acquisition** — Generate inquiries from potential customers across all business lines
3. **Investor support** — Provide credibility for investment discussions

### Secondary Goals
- Replace the 7-year-old abandoned website
- Establish the Softgrid Sensing brand (new entity name)
- Support partnership discussions (with potential SIs, resellers)

## Core Narrative Framework

### Three-Layer Messaging

**Layer 1 — Atmosphere (Hero)**
> "The AI-Powered Platform for Facility Operations"

Creates first impression. Big, confident statement about what we are.

**Layer 2 — Value (The Shift)**
> "From reactive to predictive. From people-driven to technology-driven."

Explains WHY this matters. The paradigm shift from traditional facility management to AI-driven operations.

**Layer 3 — Differentiation (Why Softgrid)**
> "Not an SI. Not a reseller. A platform company with end-to-end products, proven at national scale."

Addresses competitive positioning. We have real products, real customers, real scale.

## Content Principles

### Do's
- Lead with outcomes, not features
- Use specific numbers (10,000+ lifts, not "many lifts")
- Show, don't just tell (screenshots, diagrams)
- Speak to business value, not just technical capability
- Include social proof (logos, case studies)

### Don'ts
- Don't be overly technical for business audience
- Don't use jargon without explanation
- Don't hide behind vague statements
- Don't pretend to be bigger than we are (authenticity matters)
- Don't neglect mobile (though desktop is primary)

## Tone of Voice

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Confident** | We know what we're doing (8 years, 10,000 lifts) |
| **Professional** | Enterprise-grade, not startup-casual |
| **Clear** | Technical but accessible |
| **Forward-looking** | AI-driven, platform thinking |
| **Grounded** | Real products, real customers, not vaporware |

### Example Tone

❌ "We leverage cutting-edge AI paradigms to synergize operational efficiencies"

✅ "Our AI predicts equipment failures before they happen, reducing maintenance costs by up to 50%"
