## Company Evolution

**2017**: Founded, started with lift (elevator) remote monitoring for HDB

**2017-2024**: Evolved from lift monitoring to full-stack provider of:

- AI-driven Industrial IoT (IIoT)
- Predictive Maintenance
- Digital Twin visualization

**Current State**: Transitioning from project-based to product-based company with ARR goal

## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitor: Surbana Jurong

- 13,000 employees, 30+ years experience
- 26,000+ HDB lifts on their system
- "Smart City in a Box" platform
- **They are also a property management company** (competing with their own clients)

### Softgrid's Differentiation

1. **Not a competitor to property managers** — We sell tools TO them, not compete with them
2. **More agile** — Small company, fast response, highly customizable
3. **End-to-end** — Both hardware and software (harder to replace)
4. **Digital Twin + BMS capabilities** that Surbana doesn't offer
5. **Local expertise** — 8 years deep in Singapore market

### Other Competitors

- **Elevate Tech**: Similar IoT + predictive maintenance focus, but more software-only
- **FacilityBot / SafetyCulture**: CMMS competitors for IRIS
